Why Should I Protect My Solar Panels ?

Solar is incredibly resilient, but it’s not 100% foolproof.Over time, your panels collect dust, animal waste, and debris such as leaves or dirt. The collection of debris like this will hinder the performance of your system. Any number of issues can arise over the next 25+ years – from dust buildup to hail damage to faulty wiring.

Correcting these problems often requires paying for out-of-pocket repairs. Covering these unanticipated costs can quickly eat into your solar savings.

But with real-time monitoring and ongoing solar service plans, you’re fully protected. If something goes wrong with your solar installation, we’ll correct the issue – at no additional charge to you.
